About Me

Hello there! I'm Rommel. I have been intrigued with technology ever since the first time I saw Star Wars. I had no family or friends in IT so my pursuit was totally self-motivated. I am currently focusing on my web development career. Web development entered my life by way of a television news segment that highlighted several notable people including Bill Gates, Mark Zuckerberg, and President Obama. That segment stated that everyone should be learning to code. The emergence of the Internet, which is a product of endless lines of code has forever changed our existence. To "speak the language" will be a great advantage in this new world.

I eventually threw myself into an immersive MEAN stack development bootcamp by the name of DevMountain. That experience taught me more than I could ever have imagined. I love to code now, and can't wait to learn my next language.

My ultimate goal is to teach my kids to code.
